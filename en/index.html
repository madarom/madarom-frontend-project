Sustainable Sourcing

Agricultural Technologies

At MAD’AROM, sustainability and transparency drive everything we do. Through SmartSaha, we ensure traceable and responsible sourcing, with real-time monitoring of farming practices, climate alerts, and practical tools that empower farmers and strengthen productivity.

Empowering Rural Communities

Partnership with HARONA

Beyond production and export, MAD’AROM is committed to social impact through its strategic partnership with the NGO HARONA. Together, we support rural communities with training in post-harvest processing, reducing losses, improving quality, and increasing incomes. This initiative is complemented by access to clean water and nutritional programs that strengthen family health and well-being. By offering women and youth entrepreneurial opportunities, we foster inclusive rural economies.

South East Madagascar

Climate Resilience & Cyclone Preparedness

Each year, Madagascar is hit by several major cyclones threatening both safety and livelihoods. In the South East region, MAD’AROM strengthens resilience with agroforestry systems that protect soils, diversify crops, and secure incomes. We also implement early-warning mechanisms and practical training to reduce risks. This approach blends agricultural science, innovation, and local knowledge to turn vulnerability into sustainability, ensuring biodiversity protection and economic stability.
